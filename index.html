Weather App
A simple app that displays the currect temperature and
a 3-day weather forecast for 4 cities in Greece
Click on a button below to display the
current weather conditions for that city
and a 3-day weather forecast
${CITY}
${CURRENT SUMMARY}
At the moment:
${CURRENT TEMPERATURE}
${CITY} 3-day forecast
DAY 1
${today summary}
${today max temp}
${today min temp}
${today humidity}
DAY 2
${tomorrow summary}
${tomorrow max temp}
${tomorrow min temp}
${tomorrow humidity}
DAY 3
${day after tomorrow summary}
${day after tomorrow max temp}
${day after tomorrow min temp}
${day after tomorrow humidity}
